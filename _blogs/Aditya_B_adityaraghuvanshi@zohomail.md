Around 5 weeks ago i got an email to be an intern and learn this ViBe course . While learning could become just copy paste just like one watch a you tube tutorial but has no question book with himself to make the topic feel more intuitive. But here that wasn't the case this great opportunity provided by Prof. Sudarshan Iyengar sir was very carefully curated .Right after tutorial one will get quizzes to check their conceptual understanding. As you will move forward through this course one thing you learn - CONSISTENCY . Yes, it matters, everyone has heard it but the way course was structured it didn't feel like a hard work but rather a riddle that Raja Vikramaditya had to go through, same feeling was shared by this Aditya.

As an instructor Nandan Prabhudesai sir  has taught beautifully. His tutoring had made quizzes feel easy which wasn't the case in past quizzes I had participated into. Even though vibe course gets finished and you may not feel like you won't be able to watch content again, but guess what learning is not content here it's VIDYA . Here you get live sessions by Riya Mehta Mam which will help you guide in this YATRA!

The community provided by this internship just can't be summed up in words but yet I will try. With mentors- beginning with Prof. Sudarshan Iyengar and then with Jinal Gupta mam, Sakshi Sharma mam, Rohit Sharma sir and Nitin Sankar sir, for always interacting with me and all other interns and asking about the Vinternship journey. Also thankful to Nandan Prabhudesai sir for mentoring throughout vibe course.
